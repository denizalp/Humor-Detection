While it definitely occupied the kids for a few hours, the Legoland Discovery Centre was overpriced for what it was.  A model city, a 4D theatre, a single ride, a jungle-gym, and some lego building activities, the $90 entry (for a family of 4) is excessive.  A disappointing Legoland when you've visited the US amusement parks.  But, the kids were occupied for a few hours...
