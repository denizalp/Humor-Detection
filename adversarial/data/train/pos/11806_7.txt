After waiting with bated breath, Tuk Tuk Creekside is finally open!\n\nFeaturing the same delicious menu as the flagship location on 17th, you can now enjoy all the pad thai, soups and rice bowls in the comfort of the 'burbs.\n\nWith an abundance of seating, including a table under their signature Tuk Tuk, you have the option to dine in. The venue is very clean, thanks to the attentive and super friendly staff.\n\nThe desserts are a must try, whether it be the fried bananas (coated in coconut and sesame) or the sweet mango sticky rice!
