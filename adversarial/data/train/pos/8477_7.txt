Why am I giving Russo's Sunoco 5 stars? Because when you roll in, stay inside your car and one of the employees will come out and fill up your tank for you. Yes, I know, you're asking \"what's the big deal?\" We'll, there aren't to many full service gas stations anymore. Great when it's super cold or raining or you're on the phone. Or, if you just don't feel like getting out to pump your own gas! They also participate in Shop 'N Saves fuel savings program.
