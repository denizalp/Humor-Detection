When a place like this opens in a non-descript strip mall we are always confused as to why it's there. Answer ---- This place could open on the dark side of the moon and be popular.\n\nOyster bar, small plate decadence at it's finest.\n\nCocktails by David English. Need I say more? Jalapeño margarita is a must.\n\nFollowed Japanese influenced bites. I had the oysters Rockefeller with the foie gras & caviar. One time a truly outrageous thought process, by the chef,  deliveres.\n\nKimchee fried rice and the shrimp jalapeño hush puppies was outstanding.\n\nThis \"Other Mamas\" is a welcome surprise.
