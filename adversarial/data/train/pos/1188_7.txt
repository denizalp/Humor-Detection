We went for lunch during the summer, when the university was not in session. But even then it was evident that this is a college joint-- sport-themed and fratty. Not the place to be if you're--say-- anything over 23.\n\nOtherwise, the brats were great, the patio nice and the service ladies friendly. The fried cheese curds were ... fried and not much more, so you can probably skip those-- unless, dude, you're totally hung over because you downed 27 natty lights the night before and you need to sober up to study...Brah...
