It was only a matter of time that the Shake Shack empire moved westward. I find it a welcome addition to the New York New York hotel complex - it's a fast, affordable, delicious food option amongst the fancy options on The Strip. It's not buried away inside either, but with doors open prominently to the sidewalk and with lots of seating. Burgers and Chicago hot dogs just as good. For something specific to this location, check out their concrete creations. The jackpot was made with Mast Bros chocolate.
