A great place to grab a tea\/coffee and some pastries if you are going through St. Clair subway station shops  - upper level by the grocery store  (you can also access this cafe from the street).  They have a lot of seating for a small place - and those nice plushier chairs too!\n\nThey have a small section for some pastries - mostly muffins and things like banana bread, carrot cake etc.  They also have some subs\/sandwiches on the side but I didn't really check it out so can't comment on the prices.  \n\nI had some peppermint tea and the banana bread and sat down to play some Angry Birds and people watch from the nice large windows they have.  I wouldn't come here for the cafe itself, but if I was passing through the station I definitely would
