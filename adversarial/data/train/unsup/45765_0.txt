Came for brunch for my daughters bday. Bottomless mimosas are her favorite. So no complaining there. I will say the food was just ok. My breakfast pizza was tasty but the crust was mushy. The breakfast sandwich was very good and the breakfast nachos were also tasty.  The chicken fingers on the kids menu were too well done and the fries were not done enough.   The waitress was working herself to death  appeared understaffed. And I felt rushed as they were trying to clear my plate while I still had my fork in my hand.  I have been here before and was excited to go again but I was disappointed
