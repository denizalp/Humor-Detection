Aside from the lovely scents (Grapefruit!  French Lime Blossom!), you simply must come in here to see what has to be the world's largest scented candle.  Seriously, the thing is the size of a bucket.  I actually can't use her lotions, I discovered they make me break into a rash, but the colognes are amazing and they'll give you a free sample to take with you if you ask nicely.
