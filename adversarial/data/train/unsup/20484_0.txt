\"Your Dollar Store with More\" is not your typical dollar store.  Not only do they have a variety of dollar store items BUT they have a party central tailored area and they sell TTC tokens!  Amazing, I know.\n\nConsidering the location, one would expect prices to be out of this world, BUT they are are not.  I have spent some time wandering the aisles to see what dollar store items can full fill my daily needs.  You will be surprised how many times I have resisted temptation to buy another non-necessity item just because it was a dollar!\n\nIf you have not yet visited the dollar store, please do if you are in the Liberty Village area.  You will be greet with a smile from its friendly staff!
