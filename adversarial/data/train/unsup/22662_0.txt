Ok...I like their brunch more than their dinner. \n\nThe fried lotus blossoms, the farmers toast and the their octopus is phenomenal! The service always seems to be good, but each time I've eaten dinner there it's been lacking. It's decor is great, and I love their bar\/happy hour deals.\n\nI just feel like they need a few dishes that takes their dinner menu up a notch. If they want to be in the same neighborhood and price range as Vintner Grill then they need to step it up. (Just a touch) \n\nIf I could give this place a 3.5 star, I would...
