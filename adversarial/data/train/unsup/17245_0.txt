Beautifully shaded trail. Clean and residential. Not crowded and walkers are friendly. Leashed dogs are welcome. Poop bags and trash cans are available. Everyone seems to pick up after their dogs so far!\n\nPark in the shopping center on the southeast corner of Hualapai and Charleston, on the Cracked Egg\/The Pint side. You will see an opening into the trail at the south end of their parking lot.
