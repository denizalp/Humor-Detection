Old Style Bar\n\nRonans Oasis is a great  neighborhood hang out. Even if it's not your neighborhood. Romans offers great drinks and plenty of other activities to suit just about anyone. \n\nRomans has a unique decor--lots of NASCAR, vintage country album covers throughout the bar. Romans serves up a good variety of burgers and a pretty good selection of beers. If your ever in the area stop to socialize or simply read the walls.
