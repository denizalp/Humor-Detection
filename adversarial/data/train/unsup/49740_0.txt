Snakes and Lattes - the Board Game cafe.\n\nMy coworker was asking what this place was. She had lived in Toronto for about 5 years and had never heard of it beforehand.\n\nWhat is it?\nA place to sit down and play board games for hours and hours.\n\nWhy?\nEntertainment, socializing, food, drinks & nostalgia--- Monopoly, Hungry Hungry Hippos, CandyLand, Trivia Pursuit, and much more.\n\nWho?\n- Anyone, any age - one friend, a group of friends, a date night, family members.\nIf you feel shy, the staff are very friendly, knowledgable and helpful.\n\nFood and Drinks?\n- Sandwiches, salads, and finger foods\n- Alcoholic and Non-Alcoholic (smoothies, coffee, tea)\n\nRecommended?\nYES! Have fun :)
