Spectacular gem with a scenic falls. Provides a scenic hike along a stream. Great for birdwatching, picnics, and hiking.\n\nIn February, the park reopened with $1 million spent on the replacement of wooden stairs and 12 pedestrian bridges, trail improvements, stream restoration and erosion control.\n\nTake your camera, there are lots of opportunities to take photos.  If you start at the lower end, the trail follows a stream, and eventually you end up at a spectacular view of the falls. \n\nLeaves you remarking, \"I never knew this was here!\"
