Palm Valley Funeral Home is a family owned and operated business. The pricing is very affordable and the price for cremation is one of the lowest in the Phoenix area. The Palm Valley chapel can accomidate 80 to 90 people. After checking with area funeral homes close to Palm Valley, the cost for caskets and burial arrangements is less expensive for the exact same arrangements. The funeral home specializes in helping veterans and their families schedule services at the National Memorial Cemetery of Arizona for either casket burial or burial of cremated remains. Palm Valley Funeral Home goes the extra mile by using the most state of the art crematory, not only in the Phoenix area but in the whole State of Arizona, offering the family an option to witness the cremation to ensure that their loved one is treated with the upmost respect and that the cremated remains are those of their loved one.
