If you're looking for a good park to go hang out and have a little picnic, fun with the kids this is a very good park to just do that. This park has the usual amenities an average park have, playground, slides..etc.\n\nIt also has a tennis courts, baseball field, basketball courts and water fun for the kids. It has nice walkways for a cool stroll around the park and you can ride your bike around it to cruise the scenery. Also, parking is good with plenty of places to park your vehicle.
