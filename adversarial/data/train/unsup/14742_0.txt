Cool California-style Mexican place\n\nIf all you're looking for are fish tacos, you can't go wrong. Theirs are large and flavorful, without the pitfall of being too greasy. \n\nI tried their adobada fries, and it was too sweet for me, not that there was more than a smidgen of meat anyway. Wouldn't get it again.\n\nIt's OK, but basically a poor man's Rubio's\/Baja Fresh
