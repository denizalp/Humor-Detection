Free family fun for the holidays!\nMarc Savard, Las Vegas comedy hypnotist who performs at the V Theater at Planet Hollywood, has transformed his home into a holiday destination since 2010.  Decorating the exterior of his house with over 95,000 lights that are synced to Christmas and Top 40 tunes, visitors can come and enjoy the sights and sounds nightly from 6:00pm - 10:00pm from Thanksgiving until New Year's Eve.  \nFree parking is available at the Grace Valley Church right next door.  If you want to stay in your car, you can watch the lights and listen to the synced music by tuning to 101.3 on your FM dial.\nA sight that will put you in a jolly mood, this is definitely a place to bring your family and friends during the holidays.
