Before Chagrin Blvd became a hotspot for commercial development, all that stood on what is currently Eton was a Big Boy restaurant and a dilapidated bowling alley... that was 25 years ago! Fast forward to 2018, and that same strip if land along Chagrin has become a prime area for commercial development that continues to expand. \n\nEton has evolved over time from a barren mall dotted with a few obscure, and rather pricey shops, to a robust modern shopping complex that offers fine dining as well as quick eats and retail outlets.
