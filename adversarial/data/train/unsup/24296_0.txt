When you think of shopping in Vegas, you usually think of the outlets. However, there is another great place to shop called the Fashion Show mall. \n\nThe mall is conveniently located on the strip across the street from the Wynn casino and it's walking distance from several casinos. It's not difficult to miss, just look for the large spaceship on the strip.\n\nThe mall is large in size with a variety of stores ranging from small shops to high end stores. It also has a large food court on the upper level.\n\nThe mall is called Fashion Show mall for a reason. It's because they have fashion shows throughout the day. It is very entertaining to watch. A must see if you are visiting the mall.
