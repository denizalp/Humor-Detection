There are so many other things that Las Vegas has to offer other than casinos, gambling and buffets. If you venture out from the strip you will see the beauty of Las Vegas.\n\nOne of those hidden gems is Red Rock Canyon. Just a 20-Mile drive from the strip, you will encounter one of the most amazing places in the Vegas where you can spend a day hiking, biking and exploring the canyon. If you are limited on time, you can opt in driving the 13-Mile scenic route and take in the beautiful scenery that the canyon has to offer.\n\nThis is a definitely a place you want to visit if you are visiting Las Vegas.
