Probably the most convenient place to get filipino roast pig in the city, their whole lechon pricing is fair compared to other BBQ butcher shops. Even better is that when you order their whole lechon pig, Markjoel is convenient that you'll have it for the weekend. The lechon pig makes a great centerpiece dish for any party and especially impresses your non-filipino guests who love to take \"funny\" pictures with the swine.\n\nIn truth, you can get better quality lechon somewhere else. But doing so takes considerable research which involves asking several Filipino families whose answers will vary and directions will lead you to all four corners of the city as well as the time required for some of these places to have your order ready.
