The lobster eggs benedict are my favorite eggs benedict in the world as of right now.\n\nI was skeptical at first, but I decided to go for it as all my other friends ordered the steak and eggs.\n\nThey actually give you a large generous amount of lobster on top of buttery flakey croissants. I was stuffed.. It was definitely worth every penny. I tried their steak and eggs, no where near as good as the lobster eggs benedict.\n\nPricey, but amazing
