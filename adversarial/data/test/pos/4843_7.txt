Las Vegas is pure evil, embodying and embracing everything that is wrong with people.\n\nThe view from the window seat as you're flying in says it all.  Concrete, parking lots, pavement, strip malls, more concrete...\n\nThe moment you step off the plane, the irritating flashing lights and endless ringing and dinging of slot machines greets you.  You hardly need to walk 100 feet to start gambling.
