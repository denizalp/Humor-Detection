Oktoberfest celebration right at St Lawrence Market!\n\nFor 2 evenings, St Lawrence Market has transferred into a German Beer Hall. \n\nFree flowing beers from local: Creemore\/Thornbury and from Germany\/Poland\/Belgium.\n\nTraditional Oktoberfest entertainment and Agros' Cheerleaders are there to perform their routine.\n\nAll the participates seems to have a great time, including myself!\n\nHopefully there will be a Toronto Oktoberfest next October again!\n\nFood choices though are limited, the warm beer nuts though quite enjoyable. \n\nClosing Session on Friday ended pretty early as it needs to be cleared out by 12:30 am.......
