We just can't put on pounds fast enough, the Primanti Brothers were likely thinking when considering what to put on the menu. How can we inject the maximum amount of calories in the shortest amount of time into our customers? Their solution is Pittsburgh's most famous sandwich.......\n\nSandwiches topped with french fries, cole slaw, tomatoes and onions by request.\n\nNext up in the sandwich's evolution? Coca-cola soaked bread? Chocolate shake dressing? We'll have to see...\n\nIn the meantime, worth a try for the novelty aspect. If the concept doesn't interest you, pass it up.
