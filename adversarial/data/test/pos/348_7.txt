Cute location. They have different board games that you can play. This location also has a tiki toss hook game. The shaved ice is soft and so delicious. They have so many different flavors to choose from. Another great thing is that they offer sugar free flavors. If you want a yummy drink get the cherry sublime. For the shaved ice they have 4 different sizes to choose from.  Great place for anyone who loves shaved ice.
