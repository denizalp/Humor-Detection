Stumbled upon this place while looking for a lunch spot after 8 hours of driving across Canada. So glad we found this place on yelp. The reviews speak for themselves, this place is quaint and delicious. Don't let the location fool you, the food is beyond amazing! My husband and I had tortellini cacio e Pepe and cavatelli with rosee sauce and sausage and both were immaculate. The homemade pasta and sauces were fabulous, we just left and I want more...
