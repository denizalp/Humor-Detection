The fountains are iconic for a reason, they are the best of Vegas: showmanship and beauty and wonder.  What's not to love about them?  It's a free show, an amazing display of engineering, it's beautiful and romantic and it's a great place to stop and take a break when your heels are starting to kill halfway down the strip.  No matter what season of the year or what time of day, there is always a crowd watching the show or waiting for it to start.  That's hard to beat and no other show in Las Vegas has, (yet.)
