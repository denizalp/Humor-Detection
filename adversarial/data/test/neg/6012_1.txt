This is as good as it gets for antique\/knick-knack\/collector's items shop. The knowledge and experience of the owner (and\/or whoever helps him) is evident in the selection. I've previously lived in a town that was a tourist trap for antique stores and not one of them was this good. This is like the Buffalo Exchange of consumer goods. The only warning I can offer is that prices are commensurate to the well-selected nature of the inventory, but then there's always the well-placed negotiation...
