3.5 Stars\n\nThe chicken liver mousse appetizer was creamy, light, and tasty.  Before I knew it, the basket of carbs was empty!  The  duck crepe was savory, but could have used more duck.  Hubby said he liked the rabbit crepe. The hard cider was bubbly and refreshing.  We were seated in the patio and it was a nice place to take a break from sightseeing and enjoy a relaxing bite.
