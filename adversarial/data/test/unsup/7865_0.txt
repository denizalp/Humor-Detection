Awesome newly renovated local gas station, convenience store with made to order sandwiches, wraps, breakfast sandwiches. Tasty coffee selection, donuts, fountain drinks, and slushies. Hot dogs with toppings bar including chili and nacho cheese. Free wifi in the sitting area. You can also pick up your Redbox DVD rentals here.\n\nPart of Giant Eagle so use your Advantage card for discounts and fuel savings. Also frequent beverage card only takes like 5 purchases to earn a free one...\n\nOh yeah, they sell gas... No car wash after the renovation though.\n\nOh how I wish we had these in Seattle...
