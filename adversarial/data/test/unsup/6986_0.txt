This review is for the weekend seafood brunch. The brunch is open all day. It is $39 a person which includes unlimited mimosas, wine, and beer.\n\nThe buffet is very large with many dining options such as Italian, Asian, salad, Mexican, southern style food, and seafood. The seafood includes crab legs, shrimp, oysters, and more. The seafood was fresh and delicious.\n\nService was friendly and the restaurant was very clean.
