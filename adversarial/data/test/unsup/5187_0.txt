The Hilton Suites is great for those who are visiting Phoenix.\n\nThe rooms are clean, and the layout of rooms are well thought out. Further, the architecture is rather interesting.\n\nRoom service is average and overpriced.\n\nYou can get a rather good price for rooms if you reserve ahead.\n\nAdditional Notes:\n\n- Parking is not limited to the small area closest to the hotel, but rather, to the entire parking structure.
