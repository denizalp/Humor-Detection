They were busy but we waited a long time for our orders.\n\nPrice: definitely has room for improvement here. I feel like for a crepe without any uncommon or expensive ingredients it was pushing it.\n\nAmbiance: loud, it seems. A bit too much for my taste since I was looking for a relaxed place for a dessert after going out for dinner.
