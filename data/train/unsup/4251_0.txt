Food was okay but prepare to wait  for your food since it is made to order. \n\nThey serve a variety of  sandwhiches such as the clubhouse, BLT, turkey and bacon melt, grilled cheese as well as burgers and such. For non carnivores they have a veggie burger and salads. The deli also offer a small breakfast selection - just the basics; pancakes, oatmeal, breakfast sandwhich and fresh fruit.
