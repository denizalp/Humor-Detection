For some unexplainable reason the Wicked Spoon is one of the most popular buffets in Las Vegas. But the reviews I've seen for this place didn't reflect my experience at all.\n\nWe visited it for the evening buffet and were highly disappointed. The restaurant was very big and noisy. The food we tried was tasteless or overcooked. A lot of items I tried were not flavorful. Only the salad bar was good and had a good variety of choice.\n\nOverrated and overpriced...
