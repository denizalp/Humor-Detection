Solid pizza from a simple store front location - while you can likely eat your pie there, it's not a place you would want to hang out and linger for a long dinner.\n\nIf you go at lunch, they have a fairly standard slice and drink combo, but it's a bit on the pricey side for what you get, though you can likely make do with one slice and a drink and save the second slice for dinner, or even for breakfast the next day, so there is that.
